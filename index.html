Why Tiny Hands Play?

Tiny Hands Play started with my toddler appearing at my keyboard and urgently needing to press every key. The first game I built, Kaboom Keys, was a safe way to let her mash the keyboard without wrecking my work.

After that, I kept building. I realised a lot of kids' games were either paid, packed with ads, or just not quite right for little ones.

So I made Tiny Hands Play: a collection of simple browser games with no installs, no accounts, and no setup. Just open it, hand it over, and enjoy a few fun minutes together.

I try to add a small educational element where I can, and I want to keep it free for every family.

If it helps, bookmark it, share it with other parents, and, if you'd like, leave a small tip to help keep it running.

— A dad
