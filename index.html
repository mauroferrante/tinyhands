Kaboom Keys

6 months – 3 years

Smash the keyboard and make it rain emojis!

Press any key or tap the screen — emojis pop up with fun sounds and colors!

🧒 What they learn: Cause-and-effect thinking, fine motor skills, and sensory exploration. Every keypress gets a response, teaching little ones that their actions make things happen.

Stack It Up

3 – 5 years

How tall can you build your tower?

Tap to drop blocks and build the tallest tower. Keep them balanced or it all comes crashing down!

🧒 What they learn: Spatial awareness, timing, and patience. Kids discover how balance and gravity work through hands-on trial and error.

Spell It Out

4 – 7 years

Find the missing letter!

See the emoji and its word with a missing letter. Press the right key to fill it in!

🧒 What they learn: Letter recognition, phonics, and early spelling. Connects pictures to words, building literacy skills one letter at a time.

Why Tiny Hands Play?

I built Tiny Hands Play for those moments when a toddler appears at your keyboard and urgently needs to press every key.

It's a collection of quick little browser games with no installs, no accounts, and no setup. Just open it, hand it over, and enjoy a few fun minutes together.

I try to include a small educational element in each game where I can, and I want to keep it free forever.

So if it helps, bookmark it in your browser, share it with other parents, and, if you'd like, leave a small tip to help keep it running.

— A dad
